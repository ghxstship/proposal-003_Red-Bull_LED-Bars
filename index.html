The Unit

LED Curved
Serpentine Bar

Each unit is a self-contained, battery-ready (optional upgrade) mobile bar. Two units interlock to form a half-circle; four units close a full circle with a 30" walkway opening. Every surface glows.

What Makes It a Statement Piece

  • RGB illumination across the entire front face — remote cycles through full spectrum or locks to Red Bull red/yellow.
  • Curved serpentine form factor — visually distinct from any square-box rental bar on the market.
  • Modular footprint — 2 units for half circle, 4 for full circle with built-in staff walkway.
  • Rolls in, locks down — 3" swivel casters with brakes; removable for a flush floor setup.
  • 16" deep working surface — room for coolers, shakers, POS, and branded bar runners.
  • Ships fully assembled in a protective crate — zero on-site build time.

Lighting Technology Explained

The jump between tiers is almost entirely about the lighting engine. Here's what you're paying for.

Static LED · Tier 01

Single fixed color per unit. Chosen at order time (Red Bull Red recommended). Simple dimming and strobe. No color shifting.

RGBW · Tier 02

Red, Green, Blue + dedicated White channel. Renders the full color spectrum AND clean whites and pastels that pure RGB can't produce. Remote controlled.

DMX RGBAW · Tier 03

Adds Amber channel + professional DMX-512 protocol. Integrates with venue lighting consoles. Pixel zones enable chases, animations, and sync to music/cue stacks.
